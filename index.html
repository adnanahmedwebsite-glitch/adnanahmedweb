About Dr. Adnan Ahmed

Dr. Adnan Ahmed is a highly-skilled veterinarian with a passion for animal welfare. Graduating with honors from **Sher-e-Bangla Agricultural University**, he has a broad range of experience in both small and large animal practice.

His expertise spans from providing routine pet wellness checks and vaccinations to managing complex health issues in livestock and poultry. Driven by a commitment to the well-being of all animals, he combines evidence-based medicine with a compassionate approach.

My Expertise & Services

Pet Care

Routine check-ups, vaccinations, parasite control, and emergency care for pets including dogs, cats, and birds.

Cattle Management

Herd health management, disease prevention, reproductive services, and nutritional counseling for cattle and other livestock.

Veterinary Consultancy
